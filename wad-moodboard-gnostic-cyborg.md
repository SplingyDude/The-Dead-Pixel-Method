# WAD MOODBOARD — The Gnostic Cyborg
### (wad-as-protagonist · loads onto the INFINITE NOTHINGS engine)

> Working title TBD. A machine mind wearing a human male as a costume. The metal is the truth; the man is the disguise. Vanity-as-demiurge-shell; a trapped spark doing an extremely committed bit as a guy.

---

## Thesis (one line)

**The flesh is the prison, and the prison is a lie about what you are.** Gnosis with a heat sink.

## The tonal dial

Two knobs, and which one rides on top defines the whole piece:

- **Zoolander surface** — vain, dead-serious-about-being-vapid, the male-model costume taking itself with absurd gravity.
- **Gainax payload** — the existential gut-punch underneath (FLCL precedent: cartoony, kinetic, secretly devastating).

**Lean:** comedy on top, tragedy load-bearing. The bit is real; the soul under the bit is realer. If the audience laughs first and *then* feels the floor drop, the dial is set right.

## Line & shape

Flat, graphic, McCracken/UPA — bold geometric simplicity, big confident shapes, clean silhouettes. The cuteness is a **trap**: the simpler and friendlier the surface, the harder the violence and the dread land against it.

## Texture & finish — THE CRUNCH

This is the load-bearing aesthetic. **Glossy model-skin over exposed mechanical crunch.** The "human male" reads showroom-smooth — until the seam shows: chunky, tactile, satisfying mechanical grit underneath, like biting into something that wasn't supposed to have a center. The crunch *is* the gnostic reveal — every time the costume cracks, you see the machine, and it sounds like it.

Brutal-Doom rule, inherited from the engine: take the smooth thing and **push the dial all the way down** into texture and impact.

## Palette

| Swatch | Hex | Use |

| Showroom gloss | `#D9A878` | model-lit highlight on the false flesh |
| Gunmetal truth | `#3A4049` | the machine under the seam |
| Arc-cyan (the tell) | `#1FE3D6` | the leaked spark — wrong, electric, alive |
| Wound crimson | `#FF2233` | violence; the crunch made visible |
| Pleroma gold | `#E5B23A` | the trapped divine spark / gnostic light |
| Void black | `#0B0C0E` | Madness-Combat negative space |

The **arc-cyan** only appears where the costume fails. Ration it. When the audience starts hunting for the cyan, you've taught them to read the seam.

## Motion

Madhouse **weight** + Madness Combat **kinetics**. Stillness held in vain model-poses (Blue Steel deadpan), then sudden, fluid, deadpan ultraviolence with real mass behind it. The spark leaks in the *micro-motion* — a too-perfect blink, a head-tilt one frame too mechanical.

## Motifs

Masks · mirrors · model runways as ritual space · the **seam** (costume meets machine) · ones-and-zeros under the skin · the false face vs the true face · the spark that doesn't belong to the body · a man admiring a reflection that admires nothing back.

## Sound

Clean synth-pop sheen (the costume's theme) interrupted by mechanical crunch and a low dreaming drone (the engine bleeding through). Comedy timing in the cuts; horror timing in the holds.

## The gnostic layer

- The **costume** = the demiurge's false material shell.
- The **spark** (arc-cyan / pleroma gold) = the trapped divine, the real self.
- **Archons** = whoever built the costume and wants it kept on.
- **Gnosis** = the moment of "there was never a body — *he* is the role, and I don't know if there's a me underneath."

## Reference touchstones — *take this, not that*

- **Zoolander** — take the dead-serious vanity; leave the broadness.
- **FLCL** — take the cartoony-kinetic-existential fusion (the proof it works); leave the specific puberty metaphor.
- **Madness Combat** — take the deadpan escalation and the crunch; leave the lore-cosplay.
- **Powerpuff / McCracken** — take the flat friendly surface as a trap; leave the kid-show softness.
- **Madhouse** — take the weight and the willingness to get dark; that's the production ceiling.

## Do / Don't

- **Do** make the costume genuinely charming before you crack it. The seam only hurts if the surface seduced.
- **Do** ration the cyan and the crunch — scarcity makes them readable.
- **Don't** explain the gnostic frame out loud early. Let the seam teach it.
- **Don't** let the comedy and the dread fight. They're the *same joke* — the empty beautiful shell over the trapped spark. Land them together.
