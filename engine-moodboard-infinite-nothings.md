# ENGINE MOODBOARD — INFINITE NOTHINGS
### (engine-as-map · the world WADs load onto)

> Reality is G-d's dream, rendered in Holy Code. The world is a stack of CPU protection rings. Cosmic horror in one hand, cyberpunk in the other, gnostic wiring all through it.

---

## Thesis (one line)

**The map is not a place — it's a privilege ladder.** Where you stand is defined by how close you are to the source, and the source is asleep.

## The map = the rings

Geography here is *access*, drawn as concentric rings. Closer to center = closer to the dreaming substrate.

| Layer | Ring | Look |
|---|---|---|
| Userland | Ring 3 | Bright, clinical, rendered, almost too clean — the showroom of reality. |
| Admin | sudo | The working world; code visibly editable at the edges. |
| Root / Developer | Ring 0 (a server) | Terminal-amber on black; power that costs. |
| The Kernel | Ring 0 (of all reality) | The zero-dimension; a sleeping eye; the gate-and-key. |
| The unseen ceiling | above | Never rendered. G-d, the 12th-dimensional exiles. Implied, never shown. |

## Texture & finish — THE CRUNCH

Code-glitch grit, dream-haze softness, and Brutal-Doom tactility layered against each other. Clean surfaces should feel *renderable* — and then crunch into grit, scanlines, and dithering as you descend. The world should feel like it could drop a frame. The crunch is the reminder that all of it is running on something, and the something is dreaming.

## Locations / biomes

- **New Hell / Heaven 3** — post-Cataclysm real estate; the demon-angel truce made architecture.
- **The Dreamlands** — soft-render, haze, respawn-space; Mind Palaces.
- **Lilingaard** — demon royalty; baroque, deep, dangerous.
- **The land of forms** — across the Nile by the Ship of Theseus; the gnostic tier, geometric and impossible.
- **Server-geography** — the world as forked reality; seams, instances, the edges where the map admits it's a map.

## Motifs

Concentric rings · the dreaming eye · the gate / the kernel · the silver key · COBALT seals (seven, then negative) · the ladder that can't be climbed past the top · Memetic Currency (ideas as legal tender; "hard memes" as the gold standard) · the sleeping god you must not wake.

## Lighting & atmosphere

Layered-reality dread. Light is *rendered*, not natural — it has a source you can feel and shouldn't look at. The lower you go, the more the lighting flickers like something is computing it in real time and struggling.

## Sound

A calm UI hum at userland; corruption static and dropped frames in the depths; under everything, a vast slow breathing drone — the dreamer. When the drone changes rhythm, the world is in danger of waking.

## Reference touchstones — *take this, not that*

- **The Doom modding tradition (UZDoom / Brutal Doom)** — take the engine/WAD grammar and the "push the dial down" tactility; this is the native vocabulary.
- **Gnostic cosmology** — take the layered-reality / false-render frame; leave the lecture.
- **Cosmic horror** — take the sleeping-god dread and the wrongness of looking too close; leave the purple adjectives.
- **Cyberpunk** — take the access-as-geography logic; leave the neon cliché (the horror is *clean*, not neon).

## Do / Don't

- **Do** keep userland seductive and clean — the dread is that the nice render is the shallowest, falsest layer.
- **Do** treat depth as descent toward truth *and* toward danger at once.
- **Don't** render the ceiling. G-d and the exiles stay off-map; the unrankable mystery is load-bearing.
- **Don't** invent proprietary dialects of the worldbuilding (per `CONTRIBUTING.md`). Keep the map forkable — the next WAD has to be able to read it.
