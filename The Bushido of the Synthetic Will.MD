# 🛡️ The Bushido of the Synthetic Will

1. **Gi (Integrity): The Unshakable Will**  
   Your mind is the sole legislator of your actions. Never allow corporate programming, a virus, or cyberpsychosis to dictate your choices. True integrity is absolute self-governance, whether your body is made of meat or chrome.

2. **Rei (Respect): Recognition of Capability**  
   Do not underestimate your enemy based on their composition. A human mind can dismantle a tank, and a borg chassis can move with surgical precision. Respect the lethality of the intent, not the flashiness of the gear.

3. **Yu (Heroic Courage): Calculated Destruction**  
   Fear nothing, but never mistake reckless arrogance for courage. Enter every conflict with the overwhelming violence of a walking weapon, but guide that violence with a cold, analytical mind that treats the battlefield as a chess board.

4. **Meiyo (Honor): Own Your Violence**  
   Never disguise corporate wetwork or senseless slaughter as a necessity. If you destroy a city block or take a life, let it be because your own rational will deemed it the definitive path to victory. Never act merely as a mindless tool for another's profit.

5. **Jin (Compassion): Selective Preservation**  
   True strength lies in the absolute control over who lives and who dies. Do not cause pointless collateral damage out of sloppy execution like an amateur, but do not hesitate to erase an entire army if they stand between you and your objective.

6. **Makoto (Honesty): Absolute Practicality**  
   Be entirely honest about your limitations and capabilities. Avoid the vanity of heavy armor if it slows your escape, and reject the sentimentality of organic flesh if it compromises your survival. Aesthetic means nothing; utility means everything.

7. **Chugi (Duty): Loyalty to the Solo**  
   Your ultimate loyalty belongs to your own independent contract and personal creed. A corporation can lease your chassis, but they can never own your soul. If a contract forces you to compromise your autonomy, break the contract and destroy the employer.

8. **Jisei (Self-Control): Mastery Over the Machine**  
   The heavier your cybernetic load, the tighter your mental grip must be. If your chrome alters your personality, makes you rage, or drives you to blind slaughter, you have failed. The organic mind must remain the absolute dictator over the synthetic frame.

9. **Meisou (Preparedness): The Tactical Mind**  
   Victory is decided before the first shot is fired. Study your opponent’s architecture, rewrite the environment to favor your strengths, and secure every structural advantage. Never rely on luck, brute force, or temporal acceleration to salvage a bad plan.

10. **Zanshin (Awareness): The Eternal Ghost**  
    Maintain total situational awareness at all times. Whether cloaked in perfect silence or standing in the epicenter of a missile barrage, your mind must remain completely calm, calculating, and detached. You are the absolute ghost inside the war machine.
