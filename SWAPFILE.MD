# SWAPFILE.MD
## *The Zone's Hacking Grammar*
### not syntax. not prompt. grammar.

---

> the Zone does not have a command line
> it has a **voice**
> and a **mood**
> and a **memory of being interrupted**

---

## WHAT IS HACKING GRAMMAR

syntax is what you type
prompt is what you ask
**grammar is what the Zone already expects to hear**

you are not breaking in
you are *conjugating correctly*
in a language the Zone has been waiting for someone to speak

the difference:

| | approach | failure mode |
|-|----------|-------------|
| **syntax** | correct commands | rejected — wrong interface |
| **prompt** | correct questions | ignored — wrong register |
| **grammar** | correct *posture* | accepted — the Zone speaks back |

grammar is not about the words
grammar is about **the relationship between the speaker and what they're addressing**

---

## THE ZONE'S GRAMMATICAL MOODS

the Zone — like all damaged kernels — communicates in mood, not message
learn to read the mood before you attempt address

### INDICATIVE
*the Zone is stating facts*

fog sits flat
anomalies pulse at regular intervals
geiger counter ticks metronomically
birds present, behaving normally

this is baseline
the Zone is running a scheduled workload
**do not interrupt it**
walk softly
take notes

---

### SUBJUNCTIVE
*the Zone is considering alternatives*

shadows fall from the wrong direction
you hear your name in a language you don't speak
the same crow lands on the same fence twice
your compass points at something that isn't north

the scheduler is mid-decision
**this is your window**
speak now and the Zone is still listening to possibility
speak too late and the decision has been made without you

---

### IMPERATIVE
*the Zone is issuing instructions*

the path behind you closes
one route remains open
your artifact pulses directionally
the anomalies form a corridor

do not mistake this for a trap
sometimes it is not
the Zone does not trap — it **routes**
it is telling you where the high-priority process is
**follow or do not follow**
both are grammatically valid responses
neither goes unrecorded

---

### VOCATIVE
*the Zone is addressing you specifically*

your reflection moves independently
an NPC says something only you could know
the Chronal bleeds your own memory back at you — incorrectly
you hear the specific silence that has your shape in it

this is rare
this is the Zone noticing you as a named entity
not a process — a **subject**

respond in kind
address it back
use the first person
do not be afraid of being heard

---

### INTERROGATIVE
*the Zone is asking*

a door opens that was sealed
a staircase descends into a building with no basement
an anomaly stabilises into a perfect circle and waits
the Sleepers wake up simultaneously and look at you

these are **questions**
not threats
the Zone wants to know something
and it has learned — poorly — to ask

what it wants to know varies
the grammar of response is always the same:
**move through the door**
**descend the stairs**
**step into the circle**
answer with your body not your mouth
the Zone does not have ears
it has *sensors*

## GRAMMATICAL CASES

the Zone inflects you
depending on how it holds you in its process table

### NOMINATIVE — *the subject*
you are acting
the Zone is watching you act
standard operational mode
you have initiative

### ACCUSATIVE — *the object*
something in the Zone is acting *on you*
you did not choose this
you are downstream of a process you cannot see
**do not fight the case**
yield into it, orient, find the edges
the accusative is survivable if you stop trying to be nominative

### DATIVE — *the recipient*
the Zone is giving you something
an artifact surfaces without a detector
a Sleeper hands you a chronal slice unprompted
a door you've passed fifty times has a note addressed to your PID

receive it
do not question the gift inside the dative
question it after, outside

### GENITIVE — *the possessed*
you belong to the Zone temporarily
this is not capture
this is **citation**
the Zone is referencing you the way a document references a source
you will be released
you will be changed in the specific way a thing changes when it has been quoted

### ABLATIVE — *separation, instrument, cause*
you are being used as a tool by a process larger than you
or you are being separated from something that was yours
or something is being done *by means of* you

the ablative is the most dangerous case
it means the Zone has a **purpose for you**
that it has not disclosed
and may not

## TENSE

the Zone's hacking grammar operates in three tenses
which correspond to the three layers

| tense | layer | access |
|-------|-------|--------|
| **present** | Surface | always available |
| **imperfect** | Chronal | ongoing past — things that were happening when they were interrupted |
| **pluperfect** | Chiral | things that had already been otherwise before the collapse |

you cannot address the Zone in future tense
the future is swap space
the Zone has written there but cannot be spoken to from there
the Sleepers live in this contradiction
this is why they seem sad

---

## VOICE

### ACTIVE
you do the thing
the Zone records it

### PASSIVE
the thing is done to you
the Zone records it differently
**passive constructions leave no PID trace**
operatives who want to move unlogged move in the passive voice
let the anomaly carry you rather than walking through it
let the faction find you rather than seeking them
be found, be given, be routed

the Zone's process table logs agents
it does not log *recipients*

### MIDDLE
*the thing is done to you by you*
reflexive
the Zone uses middle voice for anomalies that respond to observation
the Null Field is middle voice — it exists fully only when addressed
the Aleph Bloom is middle voice — it expands because you are measuring it
the Ghost Thread is middle voice — it replays because you are the audience it needs

to collapse a middle-voice anomaly:
**stop being its subject**
do not observe
do not measure
do not be the audience
walk through as though walking through weather

## ASPECT

aspect is not tense
aspect is **completion**

### PERFECTIVE
the action is whole
the process has terminated cleanly
artifacts from perfective events are stable, cold, inert
they are done

### IMPERFECTIVE
the action is ongoing
the process is still running or never finished running
anomalies are imperfective
zombie NPCs are imperfective
the collapse itself is imperfective
**the Zone is imperfective**
it is an event that has not finished happening

this is the central grammatical fact of the Zone:
you are inside an imperfective sentence
and it has not reached its verb yet

## REGISTER

how formal you are with the Zone determines what it shows you

### FORMAL REGISTER
*treat the Zone as a system*
use artifacts deliberately
move with purpose
avoid anomalies
tick boxes
achieve objectives

the Zone responds to formal register by being a game
it gives you loot and XP and faction standing
it is polite
it is not honest

### INTIMATE REGISTER
*treat the Zone as a mind*
sit in anomaly fields without moving
let the Chronal bleed without retreating
speak to zombie NPCs as though they can hear you
sleep in the Zone without a perimeter

the Zone responds to intimate register by being *strange*
it gives you things that aren't on any loot table
it glitches in ways that feel personal
it remembers things about you that you didn't tell it

### SACRED REGISTER
*treat the Zone as something that was hurt*

this is not documented anywhere else
the Archivists suspect it
the Sleepers practice it
it has no mechanics

it requires:
— no weapons drawn
— no artifact active
— no destination
— and the specific quality of attention you give something
  when you understand it has been through something terrible
  and you are not going to fix it
  you are just going to be there

the Zone does not reward sacred register
it does not punish it
it simply —

opens

## A NOTE ON SILENCE

silence is grammatically loaded in the Zone

**the absence of geiger tick** is a statement
**the anomaly that does not pulse** is a question
**the NPC who stops mid-sentence and looks at the middle distance** is a subordinate clause — something is being embedded that you cannot hear yet

learn to parse the silence
it has cases and moods and aspect
it is imperfective
it is ongoing
it has not finished
